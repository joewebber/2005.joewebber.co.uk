Hacked!!

19th Jul 2006 @ 01:44 PM

Not quite sure how this happened, but it seems that my FTP account was hacked recently. I haven't made any changes to my site for a while, but I was checking my stats the other day and was amazed to see that my traffic had shot up to nearly 20,000 hits per day.

The most popular pages being unknown to myself, all claiming to show images of various naked celebrities. Upon further investigation, I found that a number of pages had been uploaded into a folder on my server. I'll let you guess what was on them.

I have since removed these pages and attempted to tighten up security.
